Frequently Asked Questions

How much is delivery?

Our standard delivery is £2.99 per order but if you live in Bedford, Wixams, Wilstead, Shortstown, Elstow or Kempston and your order exceeds £4.99, we will personally deliver it free of charge. We usually do our local deliveries on Sundays, so if you need your order earlier than than, get in touch and we'll see what we can do.
